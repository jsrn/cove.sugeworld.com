After becoming Regulars, Covian guardsmen may choose to join a Militia Squad. Militia Squads are defined and select groups of guardsmen that are trained in a particular field, rigorously improving their ability and overall combat prowess. A Squad is usually lead by a Militia officer (Sergeant or above), but this is not always necessary.
In the wide confusion of battle, Militia Squads are renowned for having the required discipline and training to stick to their roles and see the battle fought effectively. They form the core of the Militia.


Baron's Own Grenadiers
Commanding: Grenadier Sergeant Ryan Greystone
Supporting: Grenadier Corporal Maktoum
Tome: http://grenadiers.sugeworld.com/

The Baron's Own Grenadiers are the elite heavy infantry of the Cove Militia. They are usually the first into battle, placed at the vanguard of the Militia formations, and are fiercely relied upon to lead Cove to victory. Tough, hardy, and dependable, these guardsmen share strong connections with the Baron himself, and thus are sometimes tasked with special errands from Lord Octiovus.

Uniform: Full Shadow Platemail, Shadow Heater Shield, Covian Cap, Covian Sash, Covian Kilt, Boots, and Bronze Platemail Arms.

Requirements: Must be at least a Junior Guardsman. Must successfully pass Grenadiers Selection.


Light Company
Commanding: Scout Sergeant Althalus
Supporting: Scout Corporal Valiro Gryphonclaw
Tome: http://thelightcompany.cjb.net

The Light Company are the eyes and ears of the Cove Militia. Silently, they ease through the lands of Britannia, providing the Militia and the Baronship with critical intelligence on enemies (and allies). However, nay think these guardsmen are an easy ride. Light Company specialists are extremely well trained with the kryss, a fast and deadly weapon, and also have some knowledge in the art of poisoning.

Uniform: Full Green Horned Leather Jacket, Covian Cap, Covian Sash, Covian Kilt, Boots.

Requirements: Must be at least a Junior Guardsman. Must pass Scouting Course, Militia Academy.

Recommended Skills: Stealth, Hiding, Poisoning, and/or Ninjitsu.


Arcane Company
Commanding: Arcane Sergeant Arc
Supporting: Arcane Corporal Veldrin Ni'harel
Tome: http://users.cjb.net/arcanecompany/

Unlike ordinary guardsmen, the Arcane Company are the experts of all mystical knowledge. They use their abilities to aid themselves in battle, as well as aiding others. They are taught to use their abilities in conjuction with eachother to make both a formidable fighting force and a defensive squad, at the core of the Cove Militia.
Known for its clash with Church Templi, the Arcane Company is a secretive and mysterious sect; its great secrets and knowledge only being discovered by those that tread its dark path.

Uniform: Full Iron Platemail, Covian Cap, Covian Sash, Covian Kilt, Boots, and Arcane Gloves.
OR
Full Grey/White Spined Leather Armor, Ninja Jacket, Covian Cap, Covian Sash, Covian Kilt, Boots, and Arcane Gloves.

Requirements: Must be at least a Junior Guardsman.

Recommended Skills: Magery, or Necromancy.
