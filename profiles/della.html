Dellamona, Cook
Name: Dellamona Dellalany
Known As: Della
Age: 22
Gender: Female
Occupation: Cook/Tavernkeeper

Appearance
Della is around 5ft4" and she has a good posture, her hands are so small and tiny that Della almost always drops her "cookery" book, also her hands have some burns on them. She has tiny feet also , her feet are on view at times and when they are you notice they are very very small.

She has a slim body that is very well toned from lifting all the flour, she's got a slight scar on her lower back but only an intermate can see this as she always covers up.

Her hair is a fair light brown colour that draps down her shoulder, its soft and smells always of lemons. Her eyes are like stars, it is as though someone placed stars in her eyes instead. She shows alot of emoton through her eyes, so watch her carefully. Her nose is small and twitchy, she has a habit of twitching her nose when she is in danger. Her lips look so soft to touch, and they truely are, she use's bee's wax to keep them smooth.

History
She is Donwin's sister so its pretty much the same except she doesnt remember her mother, or rather she choses not too. Her father was her best friend and she was so distrote when he died.

She has never been married or had a lover infact. She wishes that her life was more like Donwin's. Della is very jealous of her sister....especially her marridge to Mhoram.
