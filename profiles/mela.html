Mela, Child
Full Name: Mela Da'Ort
Known As: Mel, Melony, Mela
Gender: Female
Age: 13 coming on 14
Work Description: Spy, Bandit

Appearance
Around 4'3 possibly a little shorter, slim with with brown hair which is normally tied in a high plait or ponytail. Her childish and straight body is slowly forming into a more womanly shape, although she still has her baby face. Across her knee she has a small scar.

Personality
A lot different from her old self, Mela is some what withdrawn. Mainly found slouching against a wall, spitting or sneering at and making snide comments at passes by.

Background
Mela was originally born into the Darion family as the sister of Lex and Dellan Darion. Not long after birth Mela was kidnapped by a drow by the name of Zilverin and his dark elf slave Larien and taught the harsh ways of the drow for five years. When Mela was finally returned to her family, it was decided Yew was not the best place for a child to grow up in, so she was sent to stay with distant relatives that lived in cove, however, news of Cove's invasion had not yet reached Yew so it wasn't known that the relative's were either long gone or dead. For week's Mela lived on her own in the forest of Cove where she finally reached the Cove Militia's barracks. Although Mela loved it in the barrack's she found it hard to communicate with adult's because of her past experience and it resulted in her becoming injured and all of her past memory wiped out. Mela now lives with who she believes is her father, Locke, ex woad raider from the Celts of Kallahar.
