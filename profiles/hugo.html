Hugo, Templar Preceptor
Name: Simply Sir Hugo to most, but his actual name is Sir Hugo of Rothermere.
Age: Hard to tell, but around his fifties.
Gender: Male

Appearance
Hugo is quite tall, but this is amplified by his aura. He has a finely trimmed vandyke, and long hair. His armour is engraved and he wears an ancient seal upon his surcoat. Around his belt he almost always carries a copy of the scriptures. His favoured weapon is his huge warhammer which he affectionately refers to as "the bane of infidels".

History
Hugo was the son of another knight, and the descended from the founder of the Covian knight order.
Hugo's mother was slain by Drow's not long after he was born and as such he never got to know his mother. His father brought him up in a very strict manner, and as such Hugo is mostly cold and to the point. He is a veteran of many battles, and know's his foes quite well. Although he strives for a better world, he live's very much by the statement "the end's justify the means." He is currently working to rebuild his order that was smashed when Orc's invaded and occupied Cove.
