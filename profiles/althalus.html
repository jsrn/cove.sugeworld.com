Althalus, Sergeant
Age: 22
Height: 6 foot 3
Hair: Dark Red
Eyes: Change between blue green and blue depending on his mood and the lighting.
Physical Appearance: Slim but muscular, with a tan from all the hours he spends outside.

History
Me, ma and pa were merchants of a poor sort so they did travel the less known routes to make the coppers rich, and as a child I were dragged to many places far and wide and many strange things I have seen... but always Cove has been me home and back here I be! I have a few tricks I picked up off of some funny foreign sorts and a few talents of the sneaky nature but rest assured they shall only be employed in the service of Cove!
